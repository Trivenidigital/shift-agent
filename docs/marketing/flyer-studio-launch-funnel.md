## Compliance Posture

- opt-in is required for direct outbound marketing.
- Prefer click-to-WhatsApp, QR codes, warm referrals, in-person demos, and opted-in business lists.
- Avoid cold bulk WhatsApp outreach.
- Always make the sender identity clear.
- Include a STOP opt-out line in outbound marketing.
- Keep outbound cadence low: one intro, one follow-up, then stop unless the owner engages.
